We are Mainly Active

We're Maggie Maude, an experienced teacher, and Adam Mitchell, a semi-professional rugby player and coach. We're passionate about sport, fitness and getting children of all ages and abilities involved.

We've also started offering fun, high impact fitness circuit classes suitable for all every Wednesday at Dance Factory Studios in Denholme, Bradford. Follow our Facebook page for more details.

What we offer

We offer a range of services including one-off PE lessons, planning and delivering of sports days, after school clubs and using sport to provide PPA cover.

We're CRB checked and certified First Aiders.

Benefits of our coaching

Our sports lessons and clubs can help improve:

  • hand/eye co-ordination;
  • teamwork;
  • motivation;
  • fitness;
  • and general motor skills.
